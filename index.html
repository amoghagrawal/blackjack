How to Play

  • The goal is to have a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.
  • Each player is dealt two cards to start.
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever is more advantageous.
  • Number cards are worth their face value.

Game Rules

  • Click "Hit" to draw another card.
  • Click "Stand" to keep your current hand.
  • The dealer must draw cards until reaching 17 or higher.

Winning Conditions

  • If your hand exceeds 21, you lose (bust).
  • If the dealer busts, you win.
  • If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's, you win.
  • If it's a tie, it's a push (no one wins).
